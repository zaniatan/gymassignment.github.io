Programs Offered

Here at Blue Gym, we provide services and programs to aid your training. We look forward to playing an essential role in your growth and develop as we provide programs such personalized training sessions and sports injuries treatment to members of all ages!

  • Personal Training

    • Our trainer will assist with your unique requirements and help with setting of realistic goals. Programs will be personalized and made to achieve your individual goal.

  • Personal Training for the Elderly

    • This one hour programme is catered for the elderly as light strength training as be proven to reduce the impact on illnesses and chronic diseases. Through this programme, the elderly can improve their balance, mobility and overall strength.

  • Group Training

    • Grab a few friends and sign up for a group training session! With more partner exercises designed for this programme, there will definitely be laughter and fun. This programme is also available to teams who are interested in booking the gym for a team session.

  • Treatment for Sport Injuries

    • Holding certificates in Dry Needling, Sports Taping and Myofascial Release, we are able to incorporate these services into our sports treatment. Aiding in the rehabilitation process, allowing you to release muscle tension and recover.
