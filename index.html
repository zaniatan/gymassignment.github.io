BLUE GYM

The Blue Gym is offers the standard range of gym equipment, ranging from deadlift sets to bench presses and
treadmills - all of which are in pristine condition! Walk-in rates are priced at $10, whereas monthly rates are
priced from $100 for members. Located at The Promenade @ Pelikat #B1-38 Singapore 537643 , the
Blue Gym is open 24 hours for members! Walk-in customers are welcomed on the weekdays from 12pm to 9pm.
